Chocolate cake

Ingredients140g butter, plus extra for the tin, 175g golden caster sugar, 2 eggs, 225g self-raising wholemeal flour, 50g cocoa powder, 1/4tsp bicarbonate soda, 250g natural yogurt; to decorate: 300g golden icing sugar sieved, 2 tbsp cocoa powder sieved, 1 tbsp butter melted, 3-4 tbsp boiling water, 50g each white plain and milk chocolate broken into squares.
Method:Step 1heat oven to 180c/fan 160c/gas 4. Butter & line the base of a 18x28cm cake tin or tray. Put the butter and sugar into a bowl; beat together with electric or hand beater till light and fluffy. Add eggs a little at a time, beating well between each addition. step 2Sieve the flour,cocoa and bicarbonate soda into the bowl, then tip in any bran left in the sieve. pour in the natural yogurt. Stir everything into a smooth mixture and spoon into the prepared tin. Bake for 20-25minutes until just firm and shrinking away from the sides of the tin.Cool in the tin for 5minutes, then turn onto a wire rack to cool completely.Step 3spread the icing over the top of the cake using a pallete knife dipped in hot water. Will keep in an airtight container for upto a week.Step 5Melt the chocolates into three different bowls in the microwave on high for a minute or over apan of simmering water. spoon the melted chocolates into three plastic disposable piping bags. snip the ends off and pipe 12 simple shapes ontop of the cake. Leave to set.

Red velvet

Ingredients150g butter, plus extra for the tin, 175g golden caster sugar, 2 eggs, 225g self-raising wholemeal flour, red food color, 1/4tsp bicarbonate soda, 250g natural yogurt; to decorate: 300g golden icing sugar sieved, red food color, 1 tbsp butter melted, 3-4 tbsp boiling water.
Method:Step 1heat oven to 180c/fan 160c/gas 4. Butter & line the base of a 18x28cm cake tin or tray. Put the butter and sugar into a bowl; beat together with electric or hand beater till light and fluffy. Add two drops of red food color. Add eggs a little at a time, beating well between each addition. step 2Sieve the flour,cocoa and bicarbonate soda into the bowl, then tip in any bran left in the sieve. pour in the natural yogurt. Stir everything into a smooth mixture and spoon into the prepared tin. Bake for 25-35minutes until just firm and shrinking away from the sides of the tin.Cool in the tin for 5minutes, then turn onto a wire rack to cool completely.Step 3separate the icing into two bowls and using one bowl ice over the top of the cake using a pallete knife put in a freezer.Take the second bowl of icing and add two drops of food color. put the red icing into a piping bag and snip the ends off and pipe 12 simple shapes ontop of the cake. Leave to set.
